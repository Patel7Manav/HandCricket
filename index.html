Rules
Based on the classic Hand Cricket Game, two players compute against each other for a 30 ball(5 Overs) showdown where the player with the maximum score at the end of game wins.
Each player has 3 wickets and if he loses 3 wickets before the end of his 30 balls, the innings comes to an end.
The scoring is pretty simple, the batsman and bowler both select a shot out of the five availabe choices. If the bowler is able to match the batsman choice, then the batsman gets out, otherwise the batsman is awarded with the runs made.
